Summary

Inception is a sci-fi thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. The film follows Dom Cobb, a thief who enters the dreams of others to steal their secrets from their subconscious. He is given a chance to have his criminal history erased as payment for the implantation of another person's idea into a target's subconscious.

Actors

  • Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur
  • Elliot Page as Ariadne
  • Tom Hardy as Eames
  • Ken Watanabe as Saito
  • Cillian Murphy as Robert Fischer
